ABOUT

Everyone has a different point of view on the world. How we look at it determines our opinions and actions and these give us our own filtered perception of reality. All of our opinions and actions are determined by our own unique filter. One which constantly changes, for the good and for the bad.

This website is a collection of my opinions and are an attempt to give a perspective to the filters which shape us. To clear our perceptions and look at reality differently. I do not intend to persuade, argue or influence, only to express. To teach beyond the uniform.
